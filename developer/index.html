The Chronicles of the Codebase

Amidst the vast repository of coding knowledge, you are tasked with maintaining the legendary "Adventure Forge," a system that transforms repositories into developer journeys. This mythical platform crafts stories from project files, engages adventurers in meaningful quests, and helps them explore unfamiliar technologies. Harnessing the guidance of advanced AI, it weaves prompts, queries, and themes into an epic experience while analyzing repositories with clinical precision. Will you master these enchanted tools to guide adventurers through their coding sagas?

Adventure Awaits – Choose your quest wisely, brave adventurer!

Choose a Quest

Uncover the secrets of code analysis in packages/core/src/analyzer/repo-analyzer.ts and packages/core/src/llm/llm-client.ts focusing on RepoAnalyzer and LLMClient functions responsible for extracting repository insights and crafting content.

Quest 4: Temple of Themes and Configuration

Explore packages/core/src/shared/config.ts, packages/core/src/shared/theme.ts, and packages/core/src/shared/input-validator.ts to master thematic control and configuration management through utilities like getAllThemes and validateAdventureChoice.
