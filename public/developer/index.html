The Realm of Repository Adventures

Long ago, legendary coders created the Realm of Repository Adventures – a magical land where teams could easily navigate complex codebases and generate immersive narratives for learning and exploration. The heart of this realm lies in an engine that analyzes repositories, validates inputs, and invokes ancient powers of AI to create adventures and quests that guide developers through every line of code. Now, it falls to you, brave adventurer, to master the tools, summon the power of themes, and uncover the magic within.

Adventure Awaits – Choose your quest wisely, brave adventurer!

Choose a Quest

Quest 1: The Tools of the Mentor

Explore the mighty RepoAdventureServer and the handler scripts that provide adventurers with tools to start, track, and complete their quests using packages/mcp/src/server.ts and packages/mcp/src/tools.ts.

Quest 2: The Forge of Quests

Learn the art of crafting stories and creating journeys by exploring the AdventureManager and StoryGenerator classes with their use of configuration files in packages/core/src/adventure/adventure-manager.ts, packages/core/src/adventure/story-generator.ts, and packages/core/src/shared/adventure-config.ts.

Quest 3: The Oracle of Code

Harness the analytical wisdom of the RepoAnalyzer and the communicative mastery of the LLMClient to dive into code analysis and content generation intricacies in packages/core/src/analyzer/repo-analyzer.ts and packages/core/src/llm/llm-client.ts.

Quest 4: The Codex of Themes

Discover and configure the magical themes and technical settings that shape adventures by exploring LLM_REQUEST_TIMEOUT, validateThemeInput, and others in packages/core/src/shared/config.ts, packages/core/src/shared/theme.ts, and packages/core/src/shared/input-validator.ts.

Quest 5: The Keeper of Configurations

Unlock the secrets of reading and formatting repository configurations for adventure generation through the utility powers hidden within packages/core/src/shared/adventure-config.ts.
