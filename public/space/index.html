Galactic Codebase Chronicles

You are aboard the Starship Repo Voyager, a spacecraft engineered to navigate the uncharted nebulae of sprawling codebases. The mission: to unravel the secrets buried within the galaxy of interdependent files and extract coherent, meaningful insights. Your starship's AI systems—powered by advanced LLM technology—serve as both navigator and interpreter of these cosmic data streams. With the fate of countless development teams depending on the code you illuminate, you must explore unique stellar systems to optimize the starship’s capabilities and fulfill your cosmic quest.
