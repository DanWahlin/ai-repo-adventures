The Pyramid of Lost Codes

In the heart of a dense jungle, shielded by vines and obscured by time, stands the Pyramid of Lost Codes. Legend tells of treasures within: tools to create new quests, analyze fragmented scrolls, and validate sacred glyphs. Brave adventurer, your journey begins to unlock these mysteries and restore the wisdom of an ancient civilization.
