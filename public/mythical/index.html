In the mystical Kingdom of Repository, a sacred artifact known as the Code Grimoire binds the kingdom together, its magic enabling knights and scholars to maintain balance across the lands. It falls to you, the destined Heroic Refactorer, to recover these enchanted pages and unify their magic. Together, they will reshape the kingdom’s destiny, granting it knowledge and wisdom anew.
